# Email Template Variations by Niche

3 variations per niche for A/B testing. Each follows the structure:
{icebreaker + bridge} + offer body + CTA

---

## Shopify/eCommerce Agencies

### Variation 1: Direct system pitch

Subject: `{first_name}, quick question about {short_company}`

```
{icebreaker + bridge}

I build outbound systems for ecom agencies. It finds DTC brand owners that need a Shopify partner, writes personalised outreach in your voice, and puts meetings on your calendar. No added headcount.

I'll put 10 qualified DTC brand owners on your calendar in the next 60 days. Brands actively running ads, real teams that need ecom growth partners. I work primarily on a results-first approach.

Can I send you a 90-sec video showing how this would work for {short_company}?

Kirsten
```

### Variation 2: Referral/network angle

Subject: `idea for {short_company}`

```
{icebreaker + bridge}

Know this is out of left field. I have a network of DTC brand owners looking for Shopify development partners. If I could put 10 of them on your calendar in the next 60 days, would you be open to a conversation?

I work primarily on a results-first approach. Would cost you nothing until meetings are landing.

Let me know.

Kirsten
```

### Variation 3: Peer/founder angle

Subject: `{first_name}, been meaning to reach out`

```
{icebreaker + bridge}

I ran an agency for 10 years. Same story every time. Deliver great work, pipeline dries up, scramble for the next client, repeat. Built an AI system to fix it for myself. Now I install it for other agencies.

It finds your ideal DTC brands, writes outreach in your voice, and books meetings. I'll put 10 qualified brand owners on your calendar in 60 days. Results-first, so the risk is on me.

Worth a quick chat?

Kirsten
```

## A/B Testing Plan

Start with Variation 1 (direct) vs Variation 2 (referral/mirror/sell angle) per niche.

| Niche | Variant A | Variant B | Split |
|---|---|---|---|
| Shopify/eCommerce | V1: Direct system | V2: Referral network | 50/50 |
| CRO/Growth | V1: Direct system | V2: Mirror expertise | 50/50 |
| Creative/Branding | V1: Direct system | V2: Shouldn't sell | 50/50 |

Run for 200 sends per variant. Kill the loser, test V3 against the winner.

```
Kirsten
```

**Macro 2: Positive with questions**
```

Yeah totally, I get this one a lot:
- {answer point 1}
- {answer point 2}
- {answer point 3}

Hopefully that helps clarify. If so, would love to chat. Really think it could work well for {short_company}. Let me know if you're open for a call {day} at {time} or {day} at {time} {timezone}. Can also send over a calendar if that works (or you can use mine: {calendly_link}).
```

```
All the best :)

```

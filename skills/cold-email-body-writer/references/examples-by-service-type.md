## Patterns across all examples

Looking at all examples above, these patterns hold:

1. **Subject line is 2-5 words, lowercase, references something specific to them.**
2. **Problem starts with "Most [audience] are [behavior]..."** This pattern triggers self-recognition.
3. **Solution always includes "We handle X, Y, Z. You just [minimal effort]."** Removes work objection.
4. **Proof always has a number.** "87 agents," "23 firms," "280 engineers."
5. **CTA offers a specific deliverable with a number.** "5 scripts," "200-prospect list," "10-page audit."
6. **Question is always 2-4 words.**
7. **Body is 80-110 words.** Never longer.
8. **Sign-off is name only or name + role.** No regards, no signatures.

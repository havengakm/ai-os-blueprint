## What goes here vs elsewhere

- Personal KB = things about Kirsten AS AN OPERATOR (her principles, voice, preferences).
- Brand context (`context/brand/`) = how the COMPANY looks and sounds (distinct from the operator, even though Kirsten is the founder).
- Company KB (`../company/`) = facts about the business offer.
- Expert KB (`../experts/`) = borrowed frameworks from named authorities.

## Skills that read from here

- Any content-producing skill that needs to write in Kirsten's voice reads `voice.md`.
- Decision-gating skills read `personal-operating.md` to check alignment with principles.

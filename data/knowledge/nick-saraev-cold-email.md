# Nick Saraev Cold Email Templates — Reference

Source: Nick Saraev's Maker School templates. Use for brainstorming additional email angles.

---

## Template 1: The Referral Angle (short, punchy)

> Hey {firstName},
>
> Know this is out of left field. If I could help you connect to people looking for PPC in exchange for a commission on closed rev (traditional referral agreement), would you be open? I work mostly on performance and have a big network.
>
> About me: used to automate brand sniping for a ~$1M/yr agency out in Vancouver, have a variety of SMB and midmarket connects. Would cost you nothing upfront.
>
> Let me know.

**Why it works:** Short, aligns incentives, has social proof. Zero risk framing.

## Template 2: The System Demo (niche-specific, detailed)

> Hey {firstName},
>
> I work with a ~$2M/yr performance marketing firm out in Calgary (dental-specific). I know you guys do PPC too, am not sure if dental, but wanted to run this by you anyways.
>
> To make a long story short: we have automated brand sniping for competitors with AI and it has been crushing. Have driven >$10M in total now, insane ROAS for clients. TLDR AI system that takes as input the URL of the page you're running ads for -> then in one click automatically finds gem keywords (old school exact match stuff) that you upload, it deletes competitors.
>
> I am trying to install this in a few other agencies because I think it has legs. Am extremely confident it'd work for you, so much so that I'd do it 100% free + automate your first campaign just to prove it. Would take just one-click and a brief call, I'll do everything else on my end within 72hrs.
>
> If you're open to it just let me know, you and a Vancouver firm are one of the first people I'm reaching out to about this. Can give you a quick ring in the next day or two.

**Why it works:** Great social proof. Niche-specific. Describes the system in plain English. Exclusivity ("you and a Vancouver firm are the first").

## Template 3: The Lead Generation Offer (outcome-based)

> Hey {firstName},
>
> I work with a ~$2M/yr performance marketing firm out in Calgary (dental-specific). I know you guys do PPC too, am not sure if for dental, but just wanted to run something by you.
>
> To make a long story short: have got a big network of people who want PPC. These come as a direct result of working with the firm + from some cold outbound I'm doing that has worked really well (kind of like this email).
>
> Would you be open to a few leads? Have driven >$10M in total now. Would guarantee you (at minimum) a bunch of booked appointments for PPC, and only if we hit a reasonable number would I ask you to pay anything. I work mostly on performance, good at what I do.
>
> Anyway, just wanted to be upfront and see if this struck a chord. Let me know and I can give you a quick ring in the next day or two.

**Why it works:** Similar to the system demo but outcome-focused. "Would you be open to a few leads?" is hard to say no to.

## Template 4: The Fan + Offer (Skool/content creators)

> Yo yo {firstName},
>
> Ngl, your content had me hooked (watched you for ~40mins yesterday lol). Extremely refreshing, so much circular/"edutainment" content in the industry rn so I appreciate you being real.
>
> Anyway: to be upfront, I have generated over $1M on Skool helping a variety of communities, mostly in the Money category. When I checked I saw you were actually crushing it very high up on the discovery page! Kudos.
>
> I know it's a bold ask, but I am 110% confident I can help you add an extra ~$20-30k rev/mo with a simple tweak. TLDR it's a really high converting welcome sequence + email course that you drip out alongside access to your course modules. It reduces churn a ton, we're talking 3-5%.
>
> Would you be open to me making you one? I work mostly on performance so it'd be a win-win. Zero work on your end, and would guarantee you a revenue bump or you'd pay $0.
>
> No pressure whatsoever, if this sounds interesting just shout. Either way I love your content!

**Why it works:** Super personalised (despite zero actual personalisation). Leads with social proof and then a killer offer. "Yo yo" and "ngl" signal human, not template.

## Template 5: The Grateful Fan + Revenue Audit

> Hey {firstName},
>
> Have been subbed to your channel for over a year. Couple of your early vids genuinely changed the path I was on. Thank you.
>
> I used to work in management consulting (in a past life). We routinely helped SMBs go from modest monthly revs like ~$20,000 to $5M/yr or more. Interesting, but soul crushing! Story for another time.
>
> Because of my experience, I can say quite confidently that you are currently losing something like -$30K/mo, primarily because you are not monetizing enough in a few classic ways.
>
> Here is my offer: do you want me to fix this for you? I would 100% guarantee I could make you at least $25,000 in the next two months. It would take next to none of your time, and I could start today.
>
> Just putting this out there as you have taught me a lot! If you're even halfway interested let me know and I can give you a ring in the next day or two.

**Why it works:** Relationship-based. "You helped me, let me help you" framing. Specific dollar amounts make the guarantee real.

---

## Key Patterns Across All Templates

1. **Performance/risk-reversal framing** — "I work mostly on performance" / "pay nothing upfront" / "guarantee or $0"
2. **Specific social proof** — "$10M total", "$2M/yr firm", "$1M on Skool" (not vague "lots of experience")
3. **Casual tone** — "yo yo", "ngl", "lol", lowercase, contractions, emoji sparingly
4. **Exclusivity** — "you and a Vancouver firm are the first", "trying to install this in a few agencies"
5. **Describe the system in plain English** — not "AI outreach platform", instead "system that finds gem keywords in one click"
6. **Always a next step** — "give you a quick ring", "send a cal invite", "let me know"

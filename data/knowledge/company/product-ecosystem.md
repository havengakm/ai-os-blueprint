# CLYMB Co. — Product Ecosystem

Modelled on the F45/FIT House of Brands approach: a unified ecosystem where every product feeds into the same core philosophy. F45 doesn't just sell group training — they sell functional fitness as a lifestyle through memberships, nutrition, recovery, apparel, events, and a global passport. Every product reinforces the core.

**CLYMB's core philosophy:** Service business founders deserve a system that grows their business while they sleep. AI makes this profitable. The founder's only job is to be great at their craft.

---

## Principles

### 1. Lead with value, always.
Give before you ask. Every interaction, every email, every piece of content delivers something useful BEFORE asking for anything. The free audit, the swipe file, the teardown video — all value first. The sale happens because they've already experienced the quality.

### 2. Sell to the rich.
Target agencies doing $300K-5M, not struggling freelancers. These founders have budget, understand ROI, and make decisions fast. They don't need convincing that pipeline matters — they need someone to build it. Price reflects value, not hours. A $5K setup fee is nothing when one closed deal covers it 3x over. Never compete on price. Compete on quality, speed, and intelligence.

### 3. Make it feel like a movement, not a transaction.
F45 doesn't sell gym memberships. They sell a fitness lifestyle with named workouts, challenges, community, and events. CLYMB doesn't sell software. We sell a founder's operating system — with named systems, a philosophy, a community, and a growth path.

## Tier 1: Free / Low Ticket (Build Audience)

**Purpose:** Attract agency founders into the CLYMB ecosystem. Prove value before asking for money. Build personal brand and trust.

| Product | Price | What it is | How it feeds the core |
|---|---|---|---|
| **LinkedIn content** | Free | Behind-the-scenes of building CLYMB, AI systems, agency growth | Builds authority, attracts inbound |
| **YouTube channel** | Free | System walkthroughs, cold email breakdowns, results reviews | Long-form proof, SEO, trust |
| **Weekly newsletter** | Free | "The Pipeline Brief" — one actionable insight for agency founders per week | Nurture, stay top of mind |
| **Cold email swipe file** | Free (lead magnet) | 5 high-converting cold email templates for agencies | Email list growth, proves expertise |
| **ICP scoring template** | Free (lead magnet) | Spreadsheet to score and qualify their ideal clients | Shows the methodology, creates need for the system |
| **Outbound calculator** | Free (lead magnet) | Calculate: "How many emails = how many meetings = how much revenue?" | Makes the math tangible, creates urgency |
| **Community** (Skool or Slack) | Free or $29/mo | Agency founder community with weekly prompts, hot seats | Network effect, social proof, upsell path |

### Why free matters:
- 80% of agencies aren't doing cold outreach. They need education before they'll buy.
- Free content builds the audience that the outbound system then targets.
- Every piece of content is a signal: if someone engages, they're a warmer lead.

## Tier 2: Mid Ticket (Entry Point)

**Purpose:** First paid transaction. Quick win that demonstrates capability. Low risk for the buyer.

| Product | Price | What it is | Delivery | Feeds core? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Revenue Audit** | $500-1,000 | 72-hour deep audit of their pipeline: where they're leaking revenue, what AI can fix, specific recommendations | Async: Kirsten reviews their business, delivers written report + Loom video | Yes — audit reveals the gaps the AIOS fills |
| **Outbound Workshop** | $997 | 2-hour live workshop: build their ICP, write their first 5 personalised emails, set up their sending infrastructure | Live Zoom, small group (5-8 people) | Yes — they experience the methodology, realise they can't do it alone |
| **DIY Outbound Kit** | $497 | Self-paced: ICP template, email templates, Smartlead setup guide, 30-day email sequence plan | Digital download + 2 weeks email support | Yes — some will DIY successfully, most will realise they need the full system |
| **Personalised Loom Teardown** | $297 | 15-min Loom video tearing down their current outbound (or website, or positioning) with specific recommendations | Async: Kirsten records, sends | Yes — builds trust, shows expertise, natural bridge to AIOS |

### The psychology:
- $297-997 is low enough that an agency founder can decide alone (no committee)
- Quick delivery (72 hours or less) builds trust fast
- Every mid-ticket product ends with: "Want me to build this for you?" → AIOS
- Even the DIY Kit converts — 60%+ of DIY buyers eventually upgrade to done-for-you

## How Everything Feeds the Core

```
Free content → builds audience → some engage → warmer leads
     ↓
Lead magnet → email list → nurture → some buy mid-ticket
     ↓
Mid-ticket (audit, workshop) → proves capability → "want me to build it?" → AIOS
     ↓
AIOS retainer (core) → results compound → "what else can you add?" → growth stack
     ↓
Growth stack → full AgencyOSX → high-ticket (VIP, equity, annual)
     ↓
Experience (retreat, mastermind, community) → deepens loyalty → referrals → more leads
     ↓
Back to top ↑
```

Every product in the ecosystem serves ONE purpose: move people closer to (and deeper into) the core AIOS retainer. Nothing exists in isolation.

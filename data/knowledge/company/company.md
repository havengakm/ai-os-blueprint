# CLYMB Co. — Company Context

## Business Overview

**Name:** CLYMB Co.
**Website:** tryclymb.com / clymbhq.com
**Founded:** 2025
**Location:** Johannesburg, South Africa (serves globally, English-speaking markets)

## What CLYMB Does

Installs AI Operating Systems for B2B service business founders. The OS is the foundation. Systems (outbound prospecting, inbound response, content, ads, reporting) layer on top.

The first system installed is always outbound prospecting (Scout) because it delivers immediate, measurable ROI: meetings booked into the founder's calendar.

## What CLYMB Does NOT Do

- Marketing agency services (ads, social media management, content creation as a service)
- CRM setup or implementation
- Website design or SEO
- Sales coaching or call training
- Lead lists without the system ("here's 5,000 emails, good luck")

## Delivery Model (Tyler Model)

1. **Onboarding ($5K setup):** Install the AIOS. Load context. Connect integrations. Solve the first problem (outbound) for instant ROI.
2. **Retainer ($2,500/mo starting):** Scout live, booking meetings. Optimise. Build next system.
3. **Growth ($3K-5K/mo):** Add 1-2 systems per month. Retainer grows as value grows.
4. **Handover option:** Full handover with training available ($10-25K). Client is never locked in.

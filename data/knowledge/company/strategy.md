# CLYMB Co. — Strategy Context

Updated: April 2026. Aligned with Top 20 Niches scoring + Offer Upgrade Playbook.

---

## Strategic Principles

1. **Sell the operating system, not the feature.** Outbound is the entry point, not the product. The AIOS is the product.
2. **Never say "AI outbound" or "AI lead generation."** Commodity language. Sell outcomes and transformation.
3. **Niche so deep that generalists cannot compete.** After 10 clients in one niche, you have proprietary data nobody can replicate.
4. **Named products per niche.** Category ownership beats generic positioning.
5. **72-hour sprint to first value.** First emails sent within 3 days, not 3 weeks.
6. **Price for value, not cost.** One closed deal from the system covers months of retainer.

### Why this order changed:

Previous order was agencies first. New order puts **Fractional Executives first** because:

- Active on LinkedIn (easy to find and reach)
- They charge $5-15K/month themselves (understand value-based pricing)

- Better to enter with proof from Tier 1 niches

## Moat Building (from playbook)

### Month 1-6: Foundation

1. **Track everything from client 1.** Standardised metrics across all campaigns in a central database, segmented by niche.
2. **Name each product.** FractionalOS, DestinationOS, AgencyOS. Landing pages within first month.
3. **Start content.** One LinkedIn post per day, rotating across niches.
4. **Document proprietary frameworks:**
   - The Bottleneck Diagnostic (assessment)
   - The Pipeline Velocity Score (metric)
   - The 5-System Growth Stack (framework)

### Month 6-12: Compound

5. **Niche benchmark reports.** "The average fractional executive using FractionalOS sees a 4.2% reply rate." Proof that sells.
6. **Vertical exclusivity.** Offer as premium tier benefit at client 3-4 per niche.
7. **Niche-specific newsletter.** Monthly insights from your data (anonymised).
8. **Community launch.** Private Slack per niche at 5+ clients.

### Month 12-24: Moat

9. **Licensing programme.** Train other operators to deliver under your methodology.
10. **Annual retreat.** Premium clients + community members.
11. **Niche podcast.** Interviewing clients and niche leaders.
12. **Proprietary data so deep no generalist can compete.**

## Sales Process

1. Cold email / LinkedIn → positive reply
2. Reply with two times + Calendly link
3. 15-min discovery call
4. Show Day 1 Value Package on the call (quick win)
5. If fit: present the named product (FractionalOS, not "outbound")
6. Show ROI math specific to their niche
7. Stack the guarantee
8. Close: 7-14 days from first reply
9. 72-hour sprint: system live within 3 days of signing

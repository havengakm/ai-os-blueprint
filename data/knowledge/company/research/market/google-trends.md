2. **New product launches (58%)** — Every new product needs early pipeline. "We just launched [thing], need to get in front of buyers fast." Cold email is the fastest channel.
3. **Quantum computing** — Not relevant now but signals where "AI" is heading. Stay informed.
4. **Physical AI (robots, drones)** — Manufacturing/logistics companies adopting AI. Future niche?
5. **Security budgets growing** — MSP/cybersecurity niche is well-timed. Companies investing in security = growing = need pipeline.

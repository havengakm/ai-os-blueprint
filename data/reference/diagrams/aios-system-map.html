BEACONcontent ghostwriter (future)  —  signal fire visible from afar, drawing others to the same path
Beacon — Content Ghostwriter FUTURE
Takes operator's themes → ghostwrites posts for LinkedIn, X, etc. Parallel track to Lead; no list dependency. Plan 4 or beyond.
Why parallel, not sequential
Beacon doesn't consume Scout's list or wait for Belay's replies. It runs on operator-supplied themes. Content amplifies the inbound side (drawing prospects to CLYMB Co) where Scout→Lead→Belay handles outbound. Together: surround-sound.

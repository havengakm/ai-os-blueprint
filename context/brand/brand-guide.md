# Brand Guide — CLYMB Co.

## Brand Name

**CLYMB Co.** — Always capitalised. Never "Clymb" or "clymb".

## Domains

- tryclymb.com (primary)
- clymbhq.com (secondary)

## Positioning

"I build AI outreach systems for service business founders."

Not: AI agency, AI automation agency, lead gen agency, marketing agency.

## Voice

- Warm, direct, casual but professional
- First person always ("I build", "I saw", "I work")
- Plain words, active voice
- Like texting a smart friend
- Growth-curious, never assumes struggle
- Confident but not arrogant
- Shows industry knowledge naturally

## Language Rules

| Say this | Never say this |
|---|---|
| team member | AI agent, AI assistant, chatbot, bot |
| system | tool, platform, software |
| does the work | automates, runs |
| meetings | conversions, demos, calls |
| your voice | tone, style, persona |
| results-first | money-back guarantee, free trial |
| installed / live | deployed, enabled, configured |

Never say: GTM, RevOps, SDR, enrichment, sequencing, deliverability (in client-facing content).

# CLYMB Co. — Business Plan

Last updated: April 2026

---

## Executive Summary

CLYMB Co. installs AI Operating Systems for B2B service business founders. The OS is the foundation. Systems (outbound prospecting, inbound response, content, ads, reporting) layer on top and get smarter over time.

**Entry point:** Outbound prospecting (Scout) — books 10+ qualified meetings per month into the founder's calendar. Immediate, measurable ROI.

**Growth path:** Add 1-2 systems per month on the same foundation. Retainer grows from $2,500 to $5,000+ as value increases.

**Founder:** Kirsten Havenga. 10+ years building client acquisition systems for 100+ service businesses across 10+ countries.

**Stage:** Pre-revenue. System built. Domain warmup in progress. First clients targeted Q2 2026.

---

## Problem

Service business founders (agency owners, consultants, fractional executives) are stuck in the feast-or-famine cycle. They're good at delivery but terrible at consistent business development.

**The data:** Cold outreach is already the #1 client acquisition channel for agencies (31% of 392 surveyed). They're doing it manually, inconsistently, and badly. 80% aren't doing cold outreach at all despite knowing they should.

**What they've tried:**
- Hired BD people ($80-150K/year, 3-6 month ramp, often quits)
- DIY cold email (10+ hours/week, inconsistent, stopped when busy)
- LinkedIn posting (audience is peers, not buyers)
- Referrals (unpredictable, uncontrollable)
- Lead gen agencies ($3-5K/month, black box, no ownership)

**The gap:** No solution gives them a system they OWN that runs WITHOUT them and gets SMARTER over time.

---

## Solution

An AI Operating System installed as infrastructure for their business.

**Layer 1 — Context:** We learn everything about their business, brand, voice, ICP, and strategy. The AI thinks like them and works like them.

**Layer 2 — Data:** Connect to their tools (CRM, email, ads, calendar). The AI reads from and writes to their actual systems. Gets deeper over time.

**Layer 3 — Systems:** Pluggable modules on top. Start with outbound (Scout), add inbound, content, ads, reporting. Each system inherits all context and intelligence.

**Key differentiators:**
1. They own everything. Every account in their name. Handover available.
2. Real personalisation (specific case studies, metrics, named clients) not template blasts
3. Signal-based timing (Reddit, LinkedIn, hiring signals) not spray-and-pray
4. Decision engine that learns from every interaction
5. One person (Kirsten) can service 4-5 clients because the AIOS makes development fast

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Prove the system (Q2 2026)
- Use CLYMB's own AIOS to prospect for clients
- SA market first (home turf, same timezone, network)
- Target: 3 paying clients by end of June
- Offer: first 3 clients get discounted/waived setup fee for case studies

### Phase 2: Scale with proof (Q3-Q4 2026)
- Case studies from first 3 clients
- Expand to US/UK markets
- Target: 8-10 clients by end of 2026
- Start A/B testing outbound angles and niches

### Phase 3: Systemise delivery (2027)
- Hire first team member (VA at 8 clients, junior at 10)
- Productise the onboarding process
- Consider partner/reseller channel
- Explore equity/profit-share model for high-value clients

### Sales process:
1. Cold email → positive reply (system does this)
2. Reply with two times + Calendly link
3. 15-min discovery call
4. If fit: proposal on the call
5. Close: 7-14 days from first reply
6. No procurement, no committee, founder decides

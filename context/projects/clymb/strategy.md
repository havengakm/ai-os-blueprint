# CLYMB Co. — Strategy Context

## GTM Strategy

### Target Market (in order of priority)

1. **South Africa** — home market, low-hanging fruit, Kirsten's network, same timezone
2. **United States** — largest market, highest ticket, most agencies
3. **United Kingdom** — strong agency scene, English-speaking, similar timezone to SA
4. **Canada / Australia / NZ** — secondary English-speaking markets

### Target Niches (validated via Reddit research, April 2026)

| Niche | Why | Deal size |
|---|---|---|
| Shopify/eCommerce agencies | High ticket, rich websites for icebreakers, clear ROI | $10-50K projects |
| CRO/Growth Marketing agencies | Data-driven, understand ROI, need pipeline | $5-15K/mo retainers |
| Creative/Branding agencies | Highest ticket, founded by creatives not salespeople | $15-50K+ projects |

### Core Offer

"I'll put 10 qualified [ideal client type] on your calendar in the next 60 days. I work primarily on a results-first approach."

No pricing in the email. No setup details. The email books the call. The call closes the deal.

### Outbound Strategy

- Cold email: 100-200/day across 6+ inboxes
- Nick Saraev methodology: personalisation, performance-based, casual tone
- 3-step sequence: Touch 1 (personalised), Touch 2 (nudge), Touch 3 (breakup)
- Multi-channel surround sound for high-intent contacts (score 50+)
- Smartlead for campaign management
- Domains warming: tryclymb.com (3 inboxes live), clymbhq.com (pending)

### Sales Process

1. Cold email → positive reply
2. Reply macro: two specific times + Calendly link
3. 15-min discovery call (Calendly)
4. If fit: proposal on the call
5. Close: 7-14 days from first reply to signed
6. No procurement, no committee, founder decides

### Positioning

- "I build AI outreach systems" — not "I'm an AI agency"
- Ownership: "Every account is in your name"
- Performance-first: "I work mostly on performance, so the risk is on me"
- Shared identity: "I ran an agency. I know the feast-famine cycle."
- Never say: retainer, manage your campaigns, AI agent, bot, chatbot

## Key Insights (from research)

- Cold outreach is already the #1 channel for agencies (31% of 392 surveyed)
- They're doing it manually, inconsistently, and badly
- "Finding consistent, quality leads without burning out" = most resonant pain point (41 upvotes)
- The offer is NOT "try cold email" — it's "the outreach you're already doing, but it runs itself"
- 80% of agencies aren't doing cold outreach. The ones who are, are grinding.

## Key Financial Metrics to Track Monthly

| Metric | How to calculate | Target |
|---|---|---|
| MRR | Sum of all active retainers | Growing month-over-month |
| Gross margin | (Revenue - variable costs) / revenue | Above 60% |
| Net margin | (Revenue - all costs) / revenue | Above 50% |
| CAC | Sales costs / new clients acquired | Under $500 |
| LTV | Average revenue x average lifetime (months) | Above $30,000 |
| LTV:CAC | LTV / CAC | Above 5:1 |
| Monthly churn | Clients lost / total clients | Under 5% |
| Revenue per hour | Total revenue / total hours worked | Above $150/hr |
| Cash runway | Cash in bank / monthly burn | Above 3 months |

## Pricing Decision Framework

### When to hold price:
- Client fits ICP perfectly
- Strong buying signal
- They came to you (inbound)
- They've tried alternatives and failed

### When to discount (max 20%):
- First 3 clients (for case studies)
- Strategic client (industry leader, referral potential)
- Multi-system commitment upfront

### When to walk away:
- Can't afford $2,500/month
- Addressable market under 2,000 contacts
- Wants guarantees beyond meetings booked (close rates, revenue)
- Procurement department or 6+ month sales cycle
- Scope creep signals during sales process
